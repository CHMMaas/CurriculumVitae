# Carolien C.H.M. Maas

**Biostatistician** | Postdoctoral Researcher at LUMC & Karolinska Institutet | Amsterdam

My background is in Econometrics, but I've been drawn toward biostatistics because I want my work to have real clinical impact. I love solving complex research questions through data analysis and statistical programming. I do most of my analyses in R, but I'm also comfortable with Python thanks to my programming and AI minor.

What excites me most about this field is collaborating closely with clinicians to make sure the work actually translates into practice. After a PhD and postdoc that were rewarding but quite solitary, I'm looking for a role where I can be part of a truly collaborative environment.

## What I work on

- **Treatment effect heterogeneity** -- understanding who benefits most from treatment
- **Prediction modeling** -- developing and validating clinical prediction models
- **Causal inference** -- estimating treatment effects from observational data
- **Cancer prognosis** -- applied work in oncology, nephrology, and cardiovascular disease
